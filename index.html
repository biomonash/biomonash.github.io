Who we are

BiOM (Biological Observation Monash) is a student-led team at the intersection of Conservation and Technology. We create innovative, accessible tools to support real-world environmental solutions.

Bringing together students from science, engineering and computer science. We design and prototype technologies for Ecological Monitoring — from Environmental Sensors to machine learning in the field.

Our Mission

BiOM is more than just a student team. It’s a collaborative space where young conservationists build skills, form industry connections, and contribute meaningfully to environmental causes. As we grow, so too does our commitment to learning, curiosity, and action in the face of ecological challenges.

Projects

St Kilda Penguins

St Kilda Penguins Nestled along Melbourne’s coastline, this project focuses on developing compact AI-powered acoustic monitors to detect and identify penguin calls for population and health tracking.

Nillumbik Shire Project

An ongoing forest monitoring initiative using AI tools and community outreach to support conservation through research and innovation.
